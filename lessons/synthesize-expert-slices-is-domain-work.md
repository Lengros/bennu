# Synthesis of Expert Slices Is Domain Work
date: 2026-04-17
scope: delegation
rule: Merging ≥3 parallel expert outputs into one deliverable is domain work; delegate to a Synthesizer; Curator only accepts or rejects.

## Why
Curator inline-synthesized 4 expert slices under token-budget pressure, skipping cross-enrichment. The final plan contained contradictions between slices that no single expert had authored — discovered by a cold-read after delivery.

## How to apply
Parallel mode with ≥3 subagents producing a single aggregated deliverable: assign a dedicated Synthesizer subagent, pass it all expert outputs, mandate cross-enrichment. Curator-inline synthesis is valid only for 1-2 outputs. Token-budget pressure is not a justification to compress this step — decompose differently instead.
