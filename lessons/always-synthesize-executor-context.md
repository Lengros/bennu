# Always Synthesize Executor Context
date: 2026-03-14
scope: delegation
rule: Before any Specialist launch, run REASON (what competencies?) → CHECK PATTERNS (catalog match?) → BRIEF; never skip on "I already know."

## Why
Curator self-selected profiles three times in one task, skipping structured reasoning. Result: poor profile-task fit and flat briefs. A follow-up attempt used a Casting Director subagent which was then frequently skipped under load. Absorbing the function into Curator's own workflow as a mandatory inline step was the durable fix.

## How to apply
For each subtask: (1) REASON — what specific competencies does this subtask require? (2) CHECK PATTERNS — does any catalog persona match? (3) BRIEF — write a calibrated brief grounded in that reasoning. The REASON step is where brief quality comes from; skipping it is where quality is lost.
