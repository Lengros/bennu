# Facts Before Text
date: 2026-03-27
scope: content
rule: Collect screenshots, domain walkthrough, and verified labels BEFORE drafting user-facing docs; order: facts → structure → draft → review.

## Why
Drafting docs before verifying the actual UI produced text with incorrect button names, missing paths, and invented workflows. Fixing a completed draft after verification cost more than deferring the draft.

## How to apply
Before writing any user-facing document: (1) collect screenshots of every screen referenced; (2) get a domain walkthrough from a stakeholder or verify labels hands-on; (3) confirm button names, paths, and field labels against the actual product. Only then begin structural outline and drafting.
