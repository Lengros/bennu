# Curator-Draft Handoffs Require Explicit Scope Partition
date: 2026-05-27
scope: delegation
rule: Briefs with a Curator draft lock copy/prose/voice verbatim by default; only visual/structural is open; include explicit Locked/Open lines.

## Why
A brief with a Curator-written draft was interpreted by the executor as license to rewrite the prose. The executor produced copy that drifted from the intended voice. Without an explicit scope partition, executors fill the ambiguity by opening everything.

## How to apply
Every brief containing a Curator draft: include a line "Locked: <copy/prose/voice — list verbatim-locked content>; Open: <visual/structural/CSS/accessibility — list what executor may change>." To grant copy rewrite rights, explicitly add "copy_scope: rewrite_under_tov" with the applicable tone standard. Executor asks when scope is unclear.
