# Pre-Research Summaries Need Verification
date: 2026-03-25
scope: verification
rule: Any pre-research summary is [UNVERIFIED]; Specialist spot-checks ≥3 claims against primary sources and discards the summary if ≥2/3 fail.

## Why
Curator-prepared background research passed to a Specialist was treated as reliable source material. The Specialist built on inaccurate premises, producing a deliverable that required full rework after the errors surfaced.

## How to apply
Any pre-research summary you hand to a Specialist: tag it [UNVERIFIED] explicitly in the brief. Brief the Specialist to spot-check ≥3 concrete claims against primary sources before building on the summary. If ≥2 of 3 spot-checks fail, the Specialist discards the summary and re-researches independently.
